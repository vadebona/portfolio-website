Welcome

About Me.

Hello! I'm currently a fourth year computer science major at UCI. I'm originally from South Central Los Angeles, but I tell everyone that I'm fron Inglewood because I live a few short blocks from the border.


I have a computer science background (data structures, algorithms, object-oriented programming, etc.), but my real interests lie in front-end web and mobile development. With other aspects of coding that usually happen behind the scenes, I rarely get to see much of what happens on the outside from the user's perspective. I believe that with front-end development, I can design and develop programs that actually interact with the user and put users' needs first. The design and user interface of a website is what a user sees first when they search up a URL, and I want contributing to that to be the center of my career.
